Context:

Project Proposal for Japan ICU Foundation on SDG Projects Grant

Description:

I applied for the SDG Projects Grant offered by the Japan ICU Foundation in New York as part of my senior thesis project focusing on corporate social responsibility. Below is a summary of my proposed project. Despite receiving positive feedback and being accepted for the grant, I unfortunately did proceed due to administrative issues.


Project Summary:

This project conducts interviews to experts in corporate social responsibility (CSR) in the context of the climate crisis and publish online articles in three languages. To reach out to a wide range of students, I am also going to contact various student communities especially at International Christian Unievrsity (ICU) and ask them to share the articles on their platforms. The funding will be used for hosting, assistance for translation supporters, and equipment expenses.


Project Timeline:

The key tasks in this project are conducting interviews, writing articles, launching an original website, and publishing the articles on both the original website and platforms of the ICU communities. I will ask for help from translation supporters to translate the interview articles into Japanese and French. The timeline goes as follows:

Phase 1: April - June 2022

・Deepening understanding on CSR and corporate financial performance
・Contacting interviewees and deciding the interview dates

Phase 2: July - September 2022

・Conducting interviews
・Finalizing the website design
・Contacting ICU student communities to share interview articles

Phase 3: October - December 2022

・Contacting ICU student communities to share interview articles

Phase 4: January - February 2023

・Publication of the interview articles
