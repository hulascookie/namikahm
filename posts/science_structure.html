Description:

Perspectives such as Semiotics, Speech Act Theory, Queer Theory, which I learned through the module, developed my analytical skills of discourse, power and knowledge in media. The main purpose of this essay is to analyze the film Beautiful Boy (2018) through the lenz of deconstruction by the Algerian-born French philosopher Jacque Derrida (1930-2004). In doing so, it clarifies that the film itself challenges the binary opposition of the sober and the addicted by exploring the intimate father-son relationship of the main characters.

What is the structure underlying language and culture? What role does language play in shaping our understanding of reality? How do binaries and hierarchies in language and thought influence our understanding of the world?


Why is this important?

・Theoretical perspectives such as structuralism and poststructuralism help us challenge seemingly fixed meanings in society
・They help us reflect our own biases and assumptions, which is crutial in developing research questions and unconventional approaches
