Namika Hamahashi — Social Science / Writing, Senses, Connecting Dots

As a student of Global Studies —an interdisciplinary field in social science, I am captivated by intersections of interests and knowledge between different stakeholders in the global society, especially confronting pressing anthropogenic challenges such as climate crises and social injustice. I strongly believe in the interconnectedness of all things, directly and indirectly, and am passionate about exploring such intersections both in empirical and theoretical manners. I like to express myself through music and connect dots by coordinating creative projects. Explore my projects further below. Check my background here.
