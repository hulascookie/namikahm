Uni-Share Vol.19

Editorials

2019, Tokyo

 

Uni-Share is a student-led community dedicated to curating ad-free "take-free" magazines, distributed at various bookstores throughout Tokyo. For Uni-Share Vol. 19, we featured artists such as Kotoha Yokozawa, Nagaho Yamamoto, Dan Isomura, Ken Kagami, Mahiru Koda, and the fashion label HOUGA.




What did I do?

I especially co-led the project "The Initial Impulse," particularly on concept development, location selection, communication and interview with the featured artist, assistance during the shoot, as well as in proofreading and layout design for the magazine.
