Namika Hamahashi — Social Science / Writing, Senses, Connecting Dots


Namika Hamahashi (she/her) recently finished her undergraduate degree in Liberal Arts and Sciences, majoring in Global Studies. This academic exploration led her to delve into the fields of international finance, public policy, media and communication theories, and more. Namika used these disciplines to closely examine her own identity and expand it to the investigation of the complexities of urban life at both micro and macro levels. In her work, she combines these disciplines to investigate questions on the intersections of climate crisis, social injustice, urban infrastructure, and sexuality.
